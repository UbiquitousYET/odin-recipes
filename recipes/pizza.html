Pizza recipe


Description

Pizza is great, everyone likes it. Everyone know what pizza is...

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato passata
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Jalapeno

Steps

  1. Put pizza dough on a baking sheet
  2. Add tomato passata
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add chicken
  5. Add Jalapeno
  6. Put pizza in the oven
