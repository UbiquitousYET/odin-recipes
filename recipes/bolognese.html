Bolognese recipe

Description

Pasta bolognese is a great dish. It can be made in a various ways. Here is one of them.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Pietruszka
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta
  • Seasoning
  • tomato passata

Steps

  1. Fry onion and garlic on olive oil
  2. Add beef
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Add carrots and pietruszka
  6. Add tomatoes
  7. Cook for some time
